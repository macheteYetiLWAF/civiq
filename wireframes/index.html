CIVIQ Wireframes v0.9

Mobile-First Design System NEPA Edition - Luzerne County

Authentication Flow
0Login
Email/password, social login (Google, Apple, X). Entry point for returning users.
0aRegister
Basic info collection. Name, email, phone, address for district lookup.
0bIdentity Verification
Multiple verification options: PA Voter Reg, Photo ID, SSN+DOB, Voter Card, Knowledge Questions.
0cSetup Complete
Welcome screen with district info, representatives preview, next election.
0dEngagement Quiz
Personalization quiz: Casual/Engaged/Power User. Topic preferences, notification settings.
Core Screens
1Home - Unified Stream
Omni-stream mixing News + Elections + Stats with filter controls. Countdown bar, weighted content based on proximity and relevance.
1aHome - Dashboard View
Alternative view with large countdown circles and election blocks. More visual/gamified dashboard approach.
1bHome - News Only
News-focused view with story accordions, bias ratings, source distribution. For users who want news-first experience.
3Leaders
Browse representatives and candidates. Funding transparency, voting record, response capability. NEPA reps included.
4Leader Profile
Individual candidate/representative view with platform, funding sources, voting record, and article responses.
5Voices
Community evaluators. Browse by level, follow, see their ratings. "Become a Voice" CTA.
6Voice Profile
Individual Voice with their evaluation history, level progression, and follower count.
7Learn
Civic education hub. Quizzes, XP progression, streaks. Constitution, government structure, your representatives.
8Article View
Individual article with multi-dimensional bias scoring, factuality rating, source analysis, and Voice evaluations.
9User Profile
Bias consumption chart, badges, voting record, XP/level, streak. Settings and Voice nomination.
10Voter Card Popup
Overlay accessible from any screen. Precinct (Ward 15), NEPA reps, next election at St. Aloysius, badges.
Extended Features
11Settings
Account, content preferences, notifications, privacy controls, engagement level toggle.
12Notifications
Activity feed: bill updates, election reminders, leader votes, Voice ratings, streak rewards.
13Bill Tracker
Track legislation. Federal, state, local bills with progress indicators and your rep's position.
14Bill Detail
Full bill analysis: summary, vote history, PA delegation breakdown, related coverage, sponsor info.
15Bias Rebalancing
Reading analysis with suggestions for diverse perspectives. 7-day balance challenge with XP rewards.
16Ghost Advisory Panel
Chat with historical figures (Lincoln, Jefferson, etc.) about current events. AI-powered perspective.
17Search & Filter
Universal search: news, leaders, bills, elections. Filter by bias, date, level, topic.
18Election Detail
Individual election view with countdown, polling place, races on ballot, key dates, candidates.
19Candidate Comparison
Side-by-side comparison: positions, experience, endorsements, funding, coverage.
20Source Comparison
Same story from multiple sources. Bias distribution, framing differences, key quotes.
21Quiz Gameplay
Daily challenge interface: questions, progress, XP earning, streak tracking, hints.

Bottom Navigation Structure

Home
Unified omni-stream. News + Elections + Stats weighted by filters. No separate tabs.
Leaders
Representatives and candidates directory.
Voices
Community evaluators and ratings.
Learn
Civic education with gamification.
