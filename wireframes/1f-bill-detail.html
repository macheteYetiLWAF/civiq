Summary
This bill provides $1.2 trillion in funding for infrastructure improvements including:
  • $550B for transportation (roads, bridges, rail)
  • $65B for broadband internet expansion
  • $55B for clean water infrastructure
  • $7.5B for electric vehicle charging
  • $21B for environmental remediation
