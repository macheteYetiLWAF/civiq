Decision Points

  • Muted/Desaturated: Keeps political metaphor but feels less partisan, more sophisticated. Still reads as "balanced" red/blue.
  • Gold Accent: Completely neutral brand identity. Gold = premium, trustworthy. Political colors only appear in bias indicators.
  • Hybrid: Best of both - gold brand identity feels premium and neutral, muted political colors only where bias is actually being shown.
